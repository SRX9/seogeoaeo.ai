- **Unlocks:** the marketing story — "an employee who keeps your brand visible"

## Goal

Give Claudia (the existing daily content agent) a second responsibility: visibility. She
audits on the plan's cadence, prepares fixes, applies the ones she's been authorized for, and
reports the score delta. Autonomy is **earned per category, opt-in** — the trust ramp from
`01-product-surface.md`. This ticket is orchestration + settings + comms; the machinery is V7.

## The three levels

- **Level 0 — Monitor (default for everyone):** scheduled audit + answer checks (V5.5) →
  findings to fix queue → digest email leading with the proof stack: "Your score moved
  61 → 68. You appeared in 4 of 10 tracked AI answers, up from 2. Clicks +9% since baseline"
  (traffic line only when V6.6 is connected) + alert email on a score *drop* ≥5.
- **Level 1 — Propose:** for `auto`-capable findings she pre-builds the fix (drafted schema,
  regenerated llms.txt, rewritten meta) and queues it for one-click approval in the fix queue.
- **Level 2 — Auto-apply:** per-category toggles; she applies without asking, logs to
  Activity, and every action is reversible (V7.2 must store the before-state). Only
  `fix_capability: auto` categories can ever be Level 2. She never touches off-site surfaces.

## Build steps

1. Cadence: read plan tier (V8.4), check last audit age, spend `visibility_audit`, run V7.3's
   re-audit. Out of credits → skip + Activity log (email already handled by V8.4).
2. Diff: compare against previous audit — new findings, resolved findings, score delta.
3. Level dispatch per finding category from `agent_autonomy`: 0 = queue only; 1 = build
   `fix_payload` now (LLM where needed) + mark "awaiting approval"; 2 = call V7.2 apply,
   record before/after.
4. Comms: one digest per audit, ordered by the proof stack — score + delta, answer share +
   delta (V5.5), traffic + AI referrals when connected (V6.6) — then top 3 new issues, what
   she fixed (against the plan's included fix cap, never a credit bill), what awaits
   approval. Merge her **writer results** into the same digest (articles published with
   their topic theses, performance-loop lines from `content-agent/C4` — one employee, one
   report). Separate immediate alert on score drops. Respect existing notification prefs.
5. Copy: everywhere she acts, name her — "Claudia fixed 3 issues this week" — per the
   content-agent narrative. Never imply she does off-site work.
6. Default state for new workspaces: Level 0 everywhere; onboarding nudge to enable Level 1
   after the first audit completes.
